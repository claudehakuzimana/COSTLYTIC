How It Works

Up and running in 5 minutes

No infra changes required. One API key to unlock complete cost intelligence.

01
Connect your providers
Add your OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google API keys in the integrations panel. We read usage data — we never touch your prompts or responses.
02
Tag your requests (optional)
Add a single metadata header to your API calls to enable per-feature or per-team attribution. Takes 2 lines of code.
03
Set budgets & alerts
Define monthly, weekly, or per-project budgets. Costlytic alerts you at 50%, 80%, and 100% usage — or at custom thresholds.
04
Optimize & iterate
Use the optimizer to identify waste, compare model costs for similar tasks, and forecast spend based on growth curves.
